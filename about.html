Following a Bachelors of Arts in Liberal Studies and a Masters of Education from Chico State University, I have been studying voiceover and acting for the last two years both privately and at the Berkeley Rep School of Theatre. There I completed the voiceover program under the wonderfully talented Sally Clawson. As I cultivated these skills I continued to take private lessons with Sally and the infinitely knowledgable Tom Chantler. Through my coursework and private training I have gained experience in a wide range of genres ranging through commercial, narration and character work.

I work from a home based studio in Alamo, CA and can accomodate turn-around as quickly as 24hrs. I would love to hear from you! drop me a line!
