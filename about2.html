Following a Bachelor of Arts in Liberal Studies and a Master's in Education from Chico State University, I studied voiceover and acting for two years both privately and at the Berkeley Rep School of Theatre. There, I completed the voiceover program under the wonderfully talented Sally Clawson. As I cultivated these skills, I continued to take private lessons with Sally and the infinitely knowledgeable Tom Chantler. Through my coursework and private training, I gained experience in a wide range of genres including commercial, narration and character work.
I work from a home based studio in Alamo, CA and can accommodate turn-around in as quickly as 24hrs. I would love to hear from you! Drop me a line!
